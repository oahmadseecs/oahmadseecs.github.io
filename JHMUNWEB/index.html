WHAT IS JHMUN?

After the great success of our first Model United Nations Conference last year, the MUN society at Jinnah Highs invites the students from your institution to participate in the 2nd Jinnah Highs MUN. Jinnah Highs School was the very first school which laid the foundation of MUNning circuit in Multan, in January 2016.This December, we are not only back bigger, but with the motive to turn the better into the best. We set new standards last year which were never seen before. Now, we will raise them all and set new ones! This is a conference targeted not just to the very experienced delegates in Multan, but even to the new MUNers to engage in a platform of innovative crisis to debate and discuss solutions on some of the pressing issues today. This three-day conference is scheduled to take place in Jinnah Highs, Multan from 3rd February to 5 February 2017 The MUN organizing committee consists of an experienced set of board members who have been to various national MUN conferences as delegates, chairs and other positions. Our pool of dedicated MUN team will bring forth the most engaging motions aimed to facilitate the highest quality of debate. They are putting everything it has into making this conference a once in a life-time experience for our participants with fruitful debates, and at the end of each day social events such as dramatics night on the first, where professionals will perform an inspirational play on the spot to entertain the delegates, concert on the second and the formal dinner and prize ceremony on the third day.

Hey y'all.All the formal greetings for you people from Jinnah Highs Model United Nations' Director General Sameer Malik. I'm quite glad to see the growing number of MUNs in Multan and feel even prouder when I recall the fact the we, the highains, were the pioneers of this event.Most of you would ask yourself that why register for a MUN? Well I firmly believe that being homo sapiens, sitting idol to what's going on in your surroundings is not an option. Before you involve yourself into a conversation of Syria Crisis and the Panama leaks, you should do your homework. Modern sciences will tell you what the world will look like in a decade, but social sciences will teach you the skill of surviving through the present. And what platform is better to practice these social sciences than a Model United Nations Conference and it gets even better when it's in your hometown. I, being the Director General, will make it sure that you don't get that 'gaijin' feel at the event. If it's your first MUN, I'll make it sure that you don't give up and loose hope. And in case you are an experienced MUNner, I'll make it sure that you don't walk out on day 3 with disappointment. See y'all soon then. Till then, Ba-bye.
-Sameer Malik, Director General External
