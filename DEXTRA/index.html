ABOUT DEXTRA

Greetings everyone! The following piece of writing will be a brief explanation of what DEXTRA is? Jinnah Highs's administration has always exhibited immense interest in promoting co-curricular activities amongst the students so that they can take a break from everyday studies and exams etc. Last year, the brilliant alumni council took the initiative of executing a science Olympiad named DEXTRA. The energetic team pulled off a great event. To continue the legacy, team DEXTRA is back with a bang. DEXTRA provides a platform to students to work as a team and make a practical use of what they learn during the academic session! There's a wide range of events to choose from. A team of minimum 3 and maximum 5 individuals can choose a minimum of 2 and a maximum of 3 events. Out of the 2 bonus events one can be chosen. The team heads have a strict criterion for elimination and qualification rounds. But obviously, you can't just walk into the campus that simply. To enter the event, there's a lengthy yet simple process to register. Once you're done with the registration process, wait for the confirmation mail and then Ta-Da, welcome to the family. Team DEXTRA isn't a group of individuals but it's a non-blood bound family which works tirelessly just so that you can make the most out of the event. Wait, wait, wait, the surprise isn't over yet. After the morning sessions of the brain-youghurting events, the night session comes like an anti-stress doze of pills. Here, in the social events, we have a fiesta night, concert night and a formal dinner (also the closing ceremony) But there are some certain restrictions too marked by the security team which are compulsory for all the participants.
So, why wait? Just register and enter! See y'all this new year.
Until then, Hasta La Vista Einsteins
